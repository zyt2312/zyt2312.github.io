§ 01About简介PROFILE个人简介

I am a first-year M.S. student in Data Science (Artificial Intelligence) at the University of Macau, advised by Prof. Derek F. Wong in the NLP2CT Lab. My research centers on the evaluation of large language models — including LLM-as-a-judge, rubric-based assessment, machine learning, and reinforcement learning — and I am also interested in agents and reward models.

I am deeply grateful to my mentor, Shudong Liu, who introduced me to research and has supported me immensely along the way. I will keep working hard to live up to the guidance and help I have received from my labmates, professors, and friends in the lab.

I am looking to pursue a Ph.D. and continue my research in academia. Please feel free to reach out via email.

§ 05Experience经历ROLES职务

Member — NLP2CT Lab, University of Macau 成员 —— NLP2CT 实验室,澳门大学 2025 – Present
  • DeepResearch RewardBench (co-first author) — built the first reward-model benchmark for long-form deep-research reports: an end-to-end pipeline over 19 agent/LLM families yielding preference datasets of 331 tasks / 866 reports, evaluation of 17 models (7B–235B), and a few-shot judge-error classifier reaching ~87% agreement with humans. Under review at EMNLP 2026.
  • CultMetric (co-first author) — a human-free, neuro-symbolic cultural-fidelity framework for machine translation, grounded in 872 culture-specific items with five pillars; validated with Wilcoxon and Spearman tests (system ranking matches experts, |ρ|<0.15 vs. mainstream metrics). Under review at ACL ARR 2026.
  • DeepResearch RewardBench(共同第一作者)—— 构建首个面向长篇深度研究报告的奖励模型评测基准:聚合 19 个智能体/大模型家族的端到端数据流水线,产出 331 任务 / 866 份报告的偏好数据集,系统评测 17 个模型(7B–235B),并提炼出与人工一致率约 87% 的 few-shot 错误分类器。投稿 EMNLP 2026,审稿中。
  • CultMetric(共同第一作者)—— 面向机器翻译的无人工、神经符号文化忠实度框架,以 872 条文化专有项为底座、设计五支柱指标;经 Wilcoxon 与 Spearman 检验验证(系统级排序与专家一致,与主流指标 |ρ|<0.15)。投稿 ACL ARR 2026,审稿中。
